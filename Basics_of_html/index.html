History of HTML

  • 1989 (Invention): Tim Berners-Leeinvents the World Wide Web at CERN.
  • 1991 (HTML Tags): He publishes "HTML Tags", a document listing the first 18 public elements.
  • 1995 (HTML 2.0): This becomes the first standardized version, adding form capabilities and standard text formatting.
  • 2014 (HTML5 Recommendation): W3C officially adopts and publishes the final HTML5 standard.
